Welcome! 

I'm Madhava. A software engineer turned engineering manager, and now I'm trying to build a company of my own. Currently, I am exploring AI agents for various enterprise applications.

Beyond work, I am still trying to figure out what it all means. There are people I love, things I care for, and some more things that I want to ignore but can't quite. Writing has always been very personal to me. It's a way for me to slow down the stream of thought in my head and try to bring some coherence to it (not very successfully, I should add). The idea of putting it all out there on the internet seems strange and intimidating.

An invisible set of eyes (even when they are not really present) brings with them some expectation. A demand for more honesty, for something a little more put together. And that's what I hope this website can provide. Please do look around and see if you find something interesting.

--  Madhava
